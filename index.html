Trustless lending on Liquid

Get a Bitcoin collateralized loan on Liquid.

Get early access

Get browser extension

We've built an awesome browser extension to facilitate trustless lending. Think of metamask, just for Bitcoin.

Get L-BTC

Get L-BTC, either via Peg-Inn (which takes 100 blocks) or using using a third party service which allows you to swap BTC for L-BTC.

Take out a loan

Lenders offer loans with various terms and conditions such as loan duration and interest rate. Pick one which suits your needs. Your L-BTC will be the collateral.

Receive L-USDT

Once you took out a loan, you receive L-USDT. You can use it to buy more L-BTC, hold it or transfer it out of Liquid using a third party service.

Pay back your loan

You will have to pay back the loan latest at its maturity. However, you can do this anytime before the loan matures.

Receive back L-BTC

Once paid back, you receive your initial collateral back in L-BTC. You can decide to take out another loan or hold on your L-BTC which you can safely store in coldstorage or keep in the browser extension.

TL;DR;

To get a Bitcoin collateralized loan on Liquid follow these steps:

  • Get our browser extension
  • Fund wallet by getting L-BTC
  • Engage in loan contract
  • Spend L-USDT as you prefer
  • Pay back loan in L-USDT
  • Receive back L-BTC collateral
