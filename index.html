Derivates on Bitcoin

Start trading CFDs on Bitcoin - no deposits and no accounts needed.

Download the client

The desktop client bundles a daemon and web-ui for your convenience. Android and iOs app are in the making.

Chose your position

Bitcoin going up? You want to open a buy position! Bitcoin going down? You want to open a sell position! Chose the CFD terms that suit you and open your position.

Proof of funds

Once the contract is negotiated you will require to provide your margin. Plug in your ledger to transfer Bitcoin to the client.

Contract setup on chain

The client sets up a DLC on the Bitcoin blockchain. There is no custodian - the Bitcoin are locked on chain until price publication by an Oracle.

Oracle price publication

Upon price publication by a 3rd party oracle you can settle the contract. Due to the oracle being a 3rd party service neither of the trading partners can cheat. If neither party settles you can refund after a timelock expires.

Receive back L-BTC

With the Oracle's signed price you can settle on chain to retrieve your profit - or simply roll over for another term to keep the contract going.

TL;DR;

To take up a CFD position using Bitcoin:

  • Download itchysats
  • Create a wallet and deposit funds
  • Engage in CFD contract
  • Wait for price to appreciate
  • Profit
